Breathing Exercise Extension Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 15 2024

The Breathing Exercise Extension ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by our extension.

Information Collection and Use:

The Breathing Exercise Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data related to your breathing exercises, including settings and progress, is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Data Storage:

Any data generated during your use of the extension (such as exercise settings) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted elsewhere.

Data Security:

As all data is stored locally, the security of this data is managed by your browser's built-in security measures. We do not have access to this data and do not implement any additional security measures.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

EasyClip Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 15 2024

EasyClip ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by EasyClip.

Information Collection and Use:

EasyClip collects and stores your clipboard data locally in your browser. This data is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties. The extension only accesses and stores the content you copy while using your browser.

Data Storage:

All data collected by EasyClip is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not have access to this data, nor is it shared with any third parties.

Data Security:

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the data that is stored locally by the extension. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
